TIPS

1. Don't think that traveling is exorbitant - it can be affordable for anybody ❤

2.Look for tickets on low cost airlines, such as RyanAir and WizzAir ❤

3. Don't take too much luggage with you, so you don't end up paying extra money at the airport ❤

4. In order to travel on budget, avoid "hotels", but rather look for hostels and free accomodation(Look at apps) ❤

5. Don't shop in the center - do shopping in residential rather than tourist areas ❤



APPS

1. WizzAir&RyanAir - cheap flight tickets in Europe and some other countries ❤

2. Couchsurfing - free accomodation and the chance to meet nice local people ❤

3. Airbnb - short-term housing ❤

4. BlaBlaCar - cheap road trips ❤

5. FlixBus - bus tickets to various destinations in Europe ❤
